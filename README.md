> **Stabilizing tourism through predictive intelligence.** An AI-powered proactive marketing engine designed to solve seasonality and connect local MSMEs to the global stage.

## 🚀 Overview

**CeView** is a destination marketing tool developed to tackle the critical issue of tourism seasonality in Cebu. 

Instead of relying on passive, reactive marketing strategies, CeView empowers local Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) with predictive analytics. By analyzing real-time global search data, the platform anticipates travel trends and automatically generates hyper-targeted promotional content, ensuring a balanced and sustainable distribution of visitors year-round.

---

## ✨ Core Features

* **Predictive Market Analyzer:** Actively monitors travel search trends across priority markets (e.g., South Korea, Japan, the USA) to detect rising interest in specific destinations or activities before the peak hits.
* **Automated Promotion (Market-Aware Publishing):** Generates market-specific promotional campaigns, including AI-tailored social media captions, visual content directions, and strategic posting schedules.
* **Weekly Performance Analysis:** Delivers easily digestible, AI-generated reports to business owners, highlighting campaign performance alongside actionable next steps to optimize future reach.

---

## 🎯 The Mission

Tourism in Cebu often suffers from heavy seasonality, leaving local businesses struggling during off-peak months. CeView was built to level the playing field. By giving MSMEs the exact same data-driven foresight used by massive travel agencies, we can proactively stimulate demand, combat the off-season slump, and foster a more resilient local economy.
